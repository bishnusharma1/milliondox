Things you probably wonder

  • Who can access my documents?

    Only the users who have been explicitly granted the access to the documents can access the database. Our “User Role Management” feature can be used to manage the access rights of the users of the information and can be changed or restricted any time.

  • Does MillionDox access, read, analyse or store my data?

    Nobody at MillionDox or any of its employees can access your information or documents. Our strong data encryption mechanism ensures that the user information remains confidential and protected from any unauthorized access and use.

  • Where is my data saved?

    MillionDox uses highly encrypted cloud servers distributed geographically as a robust storage system. There is no single server holding the entire user data thereby mitigating the risk of loss or damage through theft or other means.

  • What sets MillionDox apart from other Document Management Systems?

    MillionDox acts as a single and one of its kind document management system which also integrates the enterprise workflows claiming to enhance the process efficiency in any work environment while creating a legacy of the entire paperwork in any organisation of any size.

  • For how long will MillionDox hold my data?

    MillionDox continues to hold or retain user data for a period of 24 (Twenty Four) months from the end of your subscription. Post completion of 24 (Twenty Four) months, all user data is wiped off the servers permanently.
